Common Exercises

The following are common excerses that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily excerise routine.

Burpbee
Burprees are great, full body excerrsie to increase your strength and endurance. Begin ina standing position, drop into a squat and extend your hads forward, kick your feet back and then forward agin quickly, and ten jump up form a squatted position.
Planks
Planks build your core strength. To perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor. Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. Place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee u to your chest and then witch as quickly as you can (as though you are climbing a mountain).
